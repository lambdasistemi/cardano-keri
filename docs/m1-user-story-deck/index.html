Counterparty · public safeguards

Public checks keep Cardano and KERI in sync.

Partners do not have to take Northstar’s word for it. Public evidence tells the validator when to retire a conflicting identity or pause an out-of-date checkpoint.

Two conflicting rotations
The owner signed two different futures
Retire the identity

The validator burns the token, leaves a permanent tombstone, and pays the prover from the deposit.

KERI has moved first
A witnessed KERI rotation is newer
Pause the checkpoint

The identity can only move forward until Cardano catches up. Applications reject authority in the meantime.
